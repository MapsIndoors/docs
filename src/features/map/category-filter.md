Categories are groupings of Locations (Rooms and POIs) that you can combine in any way that makes sense for your venue.

All Locations in your MapsIndoors Solution have to be of a "Type", e.g. "Cafeteria", "Reception" or "Meeting Room". They can only be of one Type, so it is a good idea to keep them slightly generalised.

Categories can be used to put more specific Locations in a group together, e.g. Locations of the Type "Cafeteria" can be further categorised as "French Bistro", "American Grill" and "Japanese Ramen".

Lastly, a Location can also be put into multiple Categories, if applicable.
